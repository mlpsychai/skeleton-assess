Interpersonal and Personality Functioning: The convergence of elevated SAV (T=66) and INTR (T=65) scores delineates a consistent pattern of social avoidance and introversion as enduring personality characteristics. Diagnostic and Clinical Implications: The profile is most consistent with a substance use disorder, with the specific substance(s) and severity requiring further clinical evaluation.
