The MCMI-IV is a 195-item True/False self-report inventory designed to assess personality disorders and clinical syndromes in adults (18+) in clinical settings. It provides Base Rate (BR) scores derived from clinical prevalence data, with 5 Modifier Indices, 12 Clinical Personality Patterns, 3 Severe Personality Pathology scales, 7 Clinical Syndromes, and 3 Severe Clinical Syndromes. Norms are based on a clinical standardization sample.
